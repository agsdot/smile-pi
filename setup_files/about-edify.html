SMILE Plug

The SMILE-pi is a low-cost hardware solution that allows instructors to create a private micro-cloud for up to 60 users. SMILE-pi creates a portable Wi-Fi access point, gateway, and content server with which users can access SMILE Global from any device. The SMILE-pi also serves static content that can be plugged in via USB. The SMILE Plug serves the Stanford Mobile Inquiry-Based Learning Environment (SMILE) software, in addition to other available open education resources.

Several Open-Source packages are sourced from the RACHEL-Pi: http://pi.worldpossible.org/.

 

Edify

This SMILE-pi Package package is configured by Edify. Edify is a global non-profit 501(c)(3) organization committed to Christ-centered Education.

 

Seeds of Empowerment

Seeds of Empowerment contributed to the setup of this SMILE-pi Package. Seeds of Empowerment is a global non-profit 501(c)(3) organization committed to bring empowerment through education. Building upon both innovative mobile technology and educational research from Stanford University, SoE emerged in 2009 as a catalytic force to reach remote populations worldwide through mobile learning.

The SoE team partners with local underserved communities around the world to improve access and quality of education, to amplify and augment learning programs where they exist, and to create new opportunities for learning where traditional education systems are lacking or non-existent.
